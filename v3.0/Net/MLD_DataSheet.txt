
:Doctitle: MLD Peach Pit Data Sheet
:Description: Multicast Listener Discovery Protocol(MLD)

 * Peach Pit: MLD
 * Direction: Client
 
Multicast Listener Discovery Protocol (MLD) is used to declare and manage group membership for multicast addresses in IPv6.  It contains similar functionality to the Internet Group Management Protocol for IPv4. Commands include adding and removing IP's from a group as well as querying servers for current group membership.  The protocol is contained within IPv6 and its structure is similar to UDPv6.
