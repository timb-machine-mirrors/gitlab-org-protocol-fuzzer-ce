:Doctitle: IPv6 Peach Pit Data Sheet
:Description: Internet Protocol version 6 (IPv6)

 * Test Suite: IPv6
 * Direction: Client
 
Internet Protocol version 6 (IPv6) is the the communications protocol that 
provides an identification and location system for computers on networks and 
routes traffic across the Internet. IPv6 was developed to deal with the long-anticipated
problem of IPv4 address exhaustion and will eventually replace IPv4.
